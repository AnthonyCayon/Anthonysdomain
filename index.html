FPS Gaming

I’m totally obsessed with FPS games! I’ve been playing a bunch and I’ve figured out some cool stuff to help you win. First, aim for the head – it’s a no-brainer. Second, use cover wisely; don’t just run and gun. Third, listen closely; you can hear enemies sneaking up on you. Also, practice makes perfect, so keep playing and you’ll get better. And finally, don’t get too mad if you lose – it happens to everyone!
