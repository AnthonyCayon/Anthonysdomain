Tips and Tricks for FPS

Here are some tips and tricks to improve your FPS gameplay:

1. **Adjust your settings**: Lower the graphics settings to increase your frame rate.

2. **Update drivers**: Make sure your graphics card drivers are up-to-date.

3. **Optimize your system**: Close background applications to free up system resources.

4. **Check your hardware**: Ensure your hardware is functioning properly and not overheating.
