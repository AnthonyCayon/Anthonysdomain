Tips and Tricks for FPS

Here are some tips and tricks to improve your FPS gameplay:

  • Adjust your settings: Lower the graphics settings to increase your frame rate.
  • Update drivers: Make sure your graphics card drivers are up-to-date.
  • Optimize your system: Close background applications to free up system resources.
  • Check your hardware: Ensure your hardware is functioning properly and not overheating.
